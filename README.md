# 📊 TrendKart E-Commerce Sales Analysis 

---

## 📌 Project Title

**TrendKart E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard**

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing TrendKart’s e-commerce sales data using Microsoft Excel. The analysis aims to uncover key sales trends, customer behavior, and performance metrics through an interactive dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze sales performance for the year 2022
* Identify key revenue drivers (states, channels, categories)
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Generate actionable insights to support business growth

---

## 🧩 Business Problem

TrendKart wants to understand its sales performance and customer trends to improve decision-making, optimize marketing strategies, and increase overall revenue.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Cleaning
* Data Visualization

---

## 📊 Business Metrics (KPIs)

* Total Sales Revenue
* Total Orders
* Order Delivery Rate
* Average Order Value (AOV)

---

## 📈 Analysis Sections

### 🔹 Sales Analysis

* Monthly sales and order trends
* Peak sales period identification

### 🔹 Customer Analysis

* Gender-wise purchasing behavior
* Age group analysis

### 🔹 Regional Analysis

* State-wise sales contribution

### 🔹 Channel Analysis

* Performance of different sales platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.)

### 🔹 Order Analysis

* Distribution of order status (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned)

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Sales peak during **March–April** and decline towards year-end
* Women contribute approximately **64% of total purchases**
* **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** are top-performing states
* **Amazon** is the highest-performing sales channel
* Over **92% of orders are successfully delivered**
* Adults are the most active buyers

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Focus marketing campaigns on female customers
* Increase inventory in top-performing states
* Strengthen presence on high-performing channels like Amazon
* Introduce offers during low-performing months
* Promote high-selling product categories

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The project highlights key sales patterns and customer insights that can help TrendKart make data-driven decisions and improve overall business performance.
